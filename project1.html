Pain Management Animation

A 3D character-based animation designed to translate patient-reported pain experiences and treatment pathways into a clear, structured visual narrative. The project focuses on simplifying complex clinical communication while preserving accuracy, enabling patients to better understand their symptoms, treatment options, and interactions with care providers. Minimal-motion animation was intentionally used to reduce cognitive load and maintain focus on key decision points, reinforcing clarity in a sensitive and information-dense context.

About the Project

This project was created using 3ds Max for 3D modeling and animation, and After Effects for compositing and final cinematic refinement.
