Pain Awareness & Treatment Visualization

Objective

Communicate patient-reported pain and treatment pathways in a clear, structured format.

Approach

Developed a 3D character-based animation translating clinical guidance into a visual narrative. Focused on clarity and accessibility while maintaining scientific accuracy.

Key Decisions

Used minimal-motion animation to reduce cognitive load and direct attention toward key decision points. Structured scenes to emphasize patient-provider interaction and treatment progression.

Outcome

Created a visualization that improves understanding of pain mechanisms and treatment options, supporting informed decision-making.

Tools

3ds Max, After Effects
